At Employ-Me ("we", "our", or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Employ-Me is an online platform that connects job seekers with local businesses and employers, enabling users to search for local jobs, part-time jobs, and employment opportunities while helping employers find suitable workers for their businesses.

By accessing or using Employ-Me, you agree to the collection, use, and handling of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Collected from Job Seekers

When you register as a job seeker, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name (First Name, Middle Name, Last Name)
  • Email address
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Father's/Husband's Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Residential Address
  • City, State, and PIN Code
  • Highest Educational Qualification
  • Additional Education Details (if applicable)
  • Skills and Work Experience (if provided)
  • Preferred Job Locations
  • Preferred Employment Type (Full-Time, Part-Time, etc.)
  • Profile Photograph
  • Government-issued Identity Proof (such as Aadhaar Card) for verification purposes
  • Payment status and transaction information (where applicable)
B. Information Collected from Employers

When businesses or employers register, we may collect:

  • Business Name
  • Business Address
  • Business Type
  • GST Number
  • Contact Person Name
  • Contact Person Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth (used for account verification and security)
  • Business Profile Picture
  • Business Location (City, State, PIN Code)
  • Geographic Coordinates (Latitude and Longitude, where provided)
  • Payment status and transaction information (where applicable)
C. Job Posting Information

Employers may provide:

  • Job Title
  • Job Description
  • Job Type
  • Salary or Wage Information
  • Number of Vacancies
  • Job Location
  • Employment Status
  • Business Contact Details necessary for recruitment
D. Automatically Collected Information

When you use Employ-Me, we may automatically collect:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Website Usage Statistics
  • Login Activity
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies
  • Error Logs and Security Information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Verify user identity and maintain platform security.
  • Connect job seekers with suitable employers.
  • Allow employers to publish and manage job listings.
  • Allow job seekers to search and apply for jobs.
  • Display user profiles where necessary for recruitment purposes.
  • Process payments and maintain transaction records.
  • Improve search accuracy using location information.
  • Communicate important updates, notifications, and service announcements.
  • Prevent fraud, spam, fake accounts, and unauthorized activity.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. How We Share Your Information

Employ-Me does not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between job seekers and employers when necessary for recruitment.
  • With trusted payment service providers for processing payments.
  • With cloud hosting and infrastructure providers that securely operate our services.
  • With government authorities or law enforcement when required by applicable law.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of Employ-Me, our users, or the public.

All third-party service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for authorized purposes.

4. Location Information

Employ-Me uses location information to improve job matching and help users discover nearby employment opportunities.

Employers may provide business locations and geographic coordinates to improve job visibility. Job seekers may provide preferred working locations to receive more relevant job recommendations.

Location information is used solely to enhance platform functionality and user experience.

5. Identity Verification

To improve trust and reduce fraudulent accounts, Employ-Me may request identity verification documents such as government-issued identification.

Verification documents are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized personnel for verification purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Secure encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Password encryption and secure authentication
  • Access control mechanisms
  • Secure database management
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Limited administrative access to sensitive information

While we strive to protect your information, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your account
  • Provide platform services
  • Resolve disputes
  • Prevent fraud
  • Meet legal, taxation, accounting, and regulatory obligations

Upon account deletion, certain information may be retained where required by applicable law or for legitimate business purposes.

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Update your profile details.
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Contact us regarding privacy-related concerns.

Certain information may need to be retained for legal, fraud prevention, or operational reasons.

9. Cookies

Employ-Me may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Analyze website traffic.
  • Enhance security.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the platform may not function properly.

10. Children's Privacy

Employ-Me is intended for individuals who are at least 18 years of age or otherwise legally eligible to work under applicable laws.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the minimum legal age for employment. If we become aware that such information has been collected without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.
