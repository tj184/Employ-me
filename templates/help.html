Help Center

For Jobseekers
How do I create a profile?

After signing up, go to your dashboard and fill in the required details. Your profile will be reviewed by our admin team before becoming visible to employers.

Why can’t I edit some fields after saving?

To prevent misuse, critical information (name, date of birth, Aadhaar card, etc.) is locked after the first save. You can still update your skills, education, and profile picture.

For Employers
How do I hire candidates?

Complete your business profile and wait for admin verification. Once approved, you can browse verified jobseeker profiles and contact them directly.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on “Forgot Password?” on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the OTP verification steps to set a new password.
